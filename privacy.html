Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Mindshift Ventures Ltd ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit mindshiftventures.uk.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. Our website does not require accounts or personal information. The only data collected is anonymous analytics through Vercel Speed Insights:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and screen size
  • Approximate geographic region
  • Page load performance metrics

Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data through this website.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Vercel Speed Insights may use technical cookies strictly necessary for analytics.

Third-Party Services

  • Vercel — hosting and analytics. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google Fonts — font delivery. Google may collect anonymised usage data.

Data Retention

As we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data retained. Analytics data is retained by Vercel per their policies.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As a UK-registered company, we comply with UK GDPR. You have the right to:

  • Request access to any data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@mindshiftventures.uk

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
